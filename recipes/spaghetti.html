Spaghetti Recipe

Description

The following is the recipe to making a delicious spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • basil
  • garlic

Instructions

  1. boil pasta
  2. cook beef with garlic
  3. add pasta sauce and basil to the beef
  4. drain the spaghetti once cooked
  5. add the sauce to it
