Lasagne Recipe

Description

The following is the recipe to making a delicious lasagne!

Ingredients

  • lasagne noodles
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta
  • mozarella
  • parmesan
  • basil
  • garlic

Instructions

  1. boil pasta
  2. cook beef with garlic
  3. add pasta sauce and basil
  4. stir the cheese mixture in a separate bowl
  5. layer and bake until lasagne is golden brown
